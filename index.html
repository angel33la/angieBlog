About Me

Intro

My name is Angella McConnell. I am a resident of Dowagiac, Michigan. I have lived here for quite some time although, I am looking to move elsewhere. I have 7 cats who drive me crazy at times. I also have 3 grown children who all live outside of the house.

Currently I am not working anywhere. Just trying to develop the skills I need to be a web developer. I am not looking to working at any big known places like Google and/or Microsoft. I would be quite content just working at any small business and hopefully maybe one day starting my own successful business.
